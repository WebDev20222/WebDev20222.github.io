Explanation:

Lily's Limo Service will be having many updates follow its release date, but the main game is as follows.

  • You can choose to be a driver, receptionist, or guest
  • You can earn money buy working
  • Use worked money to buy limos or houses
  • There will be a starter house, but more houses to choose from later
  • Application Room within service building
  • Designated drivers who will schedule limo pickup times
  • Notifications for when the limo driver will come
  • Driver earns money buy clicking on the customer's completion button.
  • You earns more money, depending on the limos you drive people with, or limos you schedule as a receptionist
